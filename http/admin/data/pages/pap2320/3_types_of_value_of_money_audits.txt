====== 3 Types Of Value Of Money Audits ======

  - Efficiency: output vs. input.
  - Effectiveness: outcomes & ability to meet the objectives.
  - Economy: procurement at the lowest cost.

VFM is systemic, purposeful, organized, and objective examination of government activities.
  * assesses performance of policies and programs;
  * encourages accountability and best practices;
  * promotes answerable and productive government;
  * program audits; government audits; sectoral audits; follow-up audits.

__Scope__: environment; relations; protection of public assets.
