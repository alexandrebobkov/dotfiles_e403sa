====== Market & Government Failures ======

**Government failure**: inability of a public sector to secure desired economic/social objective.

**Market failure**: inefficient outcomes from private markets.

**Public Choice Theory**: rational choice theory that directly applies economic principles to political behavior.

  * bureaucrats seek to maximize personal utility in public organizations (i.e. budget formation);
  * politicians seek to maximize votes;
  * citizens attempt to maximize the receipt of government outlays;
