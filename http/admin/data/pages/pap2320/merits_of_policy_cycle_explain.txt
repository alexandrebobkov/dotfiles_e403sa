====== MERITS OF POLICY CYCLE ======
  * breaks down the complexity of the process into a number of stages;
  * can be investigated separately or in relations to other stages;
  * allows to analyse complex cases in a comparative manner;
  * can be used at any level of the government;
  * allows to determine the stages that use most of the resources.

**Policy Cycle** Agenda Setting -> Policy Formation -> Decision-Making -> Policy Implementation -> Policy Evaluation
